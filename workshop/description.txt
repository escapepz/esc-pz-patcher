[h2]Features & Fixes[/h2]
[b]ESC PZ Patcher[/b] is a technical utility designed to patch Project Zomboid's core Java classes ([code]projectzomboid.jar[/code]). It addresses several engine-level limitations and introduces specific fixes:

[list]
[*] [b]Chunk Loading:[/b] Patches the chunk downloader on login to remove timeouts, helping players with slow or unstable connections avoid the "Failed to Download Map" error.
[*] [b]Zoom Levels:[/b] Adds additional zoom levels. Ensure they are enabled in the settings. [i]Caution: excessive zooming out can significantly reduce FPS.[/i]
[*] [b]Radio Interference:[/b] Completely removes radio interference caused by range limits or adverse weather conditions.
[*] [b]PlayerCraftHistory (B42 Only):[/b] Server-side patch to prevent bloated player saves by VOIDING crafting history saves.
[/list]
